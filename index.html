The Beagle Blog

Beagle is breed of small scent hound, similar in appearance to the much larger foxhound.The beagle was developed primarily for hunting hare, known as beagling. Possessing a great sense of smell and superior tracking instincts, the beagle is primary breed is used as a detection dog for provibated agricultor imports and foodstuffs in quarantine around the world. Thebeagle is intelligent and is popular pet due to its size, good temper, and lack of inherited health problems.
